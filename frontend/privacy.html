Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services to you. This includes:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII): Such as your username, email address, and password (hashed), collected when you register for an account.
  • Event Registration Data: Information about the events you register for.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, like pages visited, time spent, and features used. This helps us understand how our service is used.
  • QR Code Content: The data (e.g., URLs, text) you input to generate dynamic QR codes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Dynamic QR Code service, including generating and managing QR codes.
  • To manage your account and provide you with access to user-specific features (e.g., "My Events").
  • To allow administrators to manage events and oversee user registrations.
  • To improve and personalize your experience on our website.
  • To analyze website usage and monitor the effectiveness of our services.
  • To communicate with you about your account, updates, and important announcements.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in limited circumstances:

  • With your consent: When you give us permission to do so.
  • For legal reasons: If required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
  • With service providers: Trusted third parties who perform services on our behalf (e.g., hosting, analytics), strictly under confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include encryption, access controls, and secure server environments. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access and receive a copy of your personal data.
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate data.
  • The right to request deletion of your personal data.
  • The right to object to the processing of your data.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
